    Instructor Profile


    CSKC BlackBelt: Mr. Rito Fernandez


    Mr. Fernandez joined the CSKC in the early 90s as a child and has maintained his commitment to

    the art of karate-do. Mr. Fernadez has represented Belzie in several international competitions,

    Such as the Central American Championships and Central American and Caribbean Games in

    Colombia and has won several National karate medals in Kata. He has now formed the Kazuko

    Shotokan karate Club which is located on Awe Street, one block form CET in San Ignacio. He was

    an Excellent Student and is now an excellent Instructor!! We are proud to endore his club and invite

    the residents of the twin towns of San Ignacio and Santa Elena to keep Karate Alive!

    Arigato gozaimasu!!
